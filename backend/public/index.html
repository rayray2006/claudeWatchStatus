Four states. Different taps.

Each state corresponds to a moment in your agent's life. The two that matter most — when it finishes and when it needs you — buzz your wrist.

Idle

No agent activity. Watch face stays calm.

Working

Agent is busy. Silent update — no buzz.

Done

Turn finished. Wrist tap so you can come back.

Approval

Agent is waiting on you. Wrist tap to act.
